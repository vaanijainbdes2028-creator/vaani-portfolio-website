The Great Coffee Conspiracy

This poster blends mystery with everyday coffee culture, using warm earthy tones and vintage illustration to create a cinematic feel. The concept revolves around the idea of uncovering "secrets" behind coffee, making it both intriguing and playful. The typography and layout add to the retro aesthetic, enhancing the storytelling aspect.

Role: Branding, Illustration, Typography
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Educational Book

*Ollie the Octopus* is a children's story designed for ages 3–6 to teach emotional awareness in a simple and relatable way. Through his underwater adventures, Ollie meets friends who feel nervous, angry, scared, sad, and excited. By offering simple ways to manage these emotions—like deep breathing, wiggling, and talking—Ollie teaches kids that all feelings are normal.

Role: Storytelling, Illustration, Character Design
Tools: Procreate, Adobe InDesign

Typography Cup

Inspired by the fast-paced energy of Mumbai, this artwork uses typography as the main design element to form the shape of a cup. Each word captures the city's essence—its chaos, fun, and vibrancy—through dynamic font placement and bold contrasts. It's a visual celebration of how design can represent culture and rhythm without using imagery.

Role: Typography, Conceptual Design, Layout
Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Playful Fonts Booklet Design

The Playful Fonts Booklet was designed to help content creators who make books for children aged 3–5 choose the right typefaces. The project identified key needs and challenges in font selection. The final booklet features 8-9 themed pages showcasing playful, legible fonts with bright colors and large layouts that mimic children's books.

Role: Research, Layout Design, Typography
Tools: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator
